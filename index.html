Shouju Wang

Ph.D. Student in Computer Science

I am a first-year CS Ph.D. student at the University of Hawai'i at Mānoa, advised by Prof. Haopeng Zhang. I received my B.Eng. from Wuhan University (Hongyi Honor College). Previously, I was a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia.

My research focuses on LLM privacy, agent alignment, and trustworthy AI. I am particularly interested in contextual integrity for multimodal LLM agents and building safe, privacy-aware AI systems.
