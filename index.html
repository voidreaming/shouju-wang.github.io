Shouju Wang

Ph.D. Student in Computer Science

I'm a Computer Science Ph.D. student at the University of 🌴 Hawai'i at Mānoa. I'm grateful to be advised by Prof. Haopeng Zhang. My research focuses on LLM Privacy, Contextual Integrity, and Agent Alignment, with a particular interest in multimodal contextual integrity for LLM/VLM agents.

Previously, I did my undergrad in CS (Honors) at Wuhan University (Hongyi Honor College). I was fortunate to work as a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia (Social Computing & DKI Groups), where I worked on realistic privacy mitigation for LLM agents and intelligent meeting-time recommendation for Microsoft Teams. I also spent time as a research assistant at Duke Kunshan University, working on fair graph anomaly detection.
